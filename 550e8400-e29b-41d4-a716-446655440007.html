VOID

  Explore new frontiers with VOID, a fantasy game console and computer in the same category as the popular PICO-8, LIKO-12, TIC-80 and WASM-4. Developed with the same framework as LIKO-12, VOID offers a unique Lua programming experience, combined with its own API. With a 256x128 pixel screen and a 16-color palette, VOID allows gamers to create and share their own games and applications using cartridges in the ".void" or ".void.png" format. With controller, keyboard, mouse, and touchscreen support, VOID is available for Windows, Linux, MacOS, Android and iOS, offering endless possibilities for just US$18.
